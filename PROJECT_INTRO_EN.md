# Job Assistant

Job Assistant is a local-first personal job-search workbench. It helps users maintain a profile, scan job sources by region, follow target companies, rank jobs against resume and preference signals, prepare application materials, and keep a traceable application tracker.

## Core Idea

Most job searches are scattered across job boards, company career pages, resumes, notes, cover letters, and trackers. Job Assistant brings those pieces into one daily workflow while keeping the final application decision human-controlled.

## Current Scope

- Multi-region personal profile: Singapore, China Mainland, and Hong Kong.
- Company radar with built-in employer catalogs and custom career-page links.
- Daily scanning with source-level status and failure visibility.
- Resume upload and local-first career-fit analysis.
- 5.0 base scoring with preference, region, location, and watched-company ranking signals.
- Apply / Watch / Drop decisions.
- Browser-assisted form filling that stops before final submission.
- Local SQLite as source of truth.

## Boundary

This is a single-user local app. It does not include accounts, cloud sync, or automatic final application submission.
